About us


  • - Bro's Kafe was founded in June 2018 by 2 Bros sharing the same passion of opening a rendezvous spot for expats in Da Nang City. - Bro's Kafe serves Specialty Coffee 100% from coffee beans with honey-red processed. - Bro's Kafe is also well-known for good sandwiches, baguette, croissant and salad that have good value for quality and casual price. - Bro's Kafe is the first coffeeshop in Da Nang that serve Green Smoothie from spinach and fresh fruits - Bro's Kafe cooperate with Bellany Ice Cream to provide high quality ice-cream with many amazing flavour such as sorbet, salted caramel Bro's Kafe is proving that high value can be made from the simple.
